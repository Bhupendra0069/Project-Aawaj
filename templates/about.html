About AAWAJ

AAWAJ (आवाज) means "Voice" in Nepali. We are an AI-powered civic complaint platform built specifically for Kathmandu Valley, Nepal. Our mission is to bridge the gap between citizens and government by making complaint reporting easy, transparent, and actionable.

🎯
Our Mission
Give every citizen a digital voice to report civic issues and hold authorities accountable.
🤖
AI-Powered
Our AI automatically classifies complaints, detects urgency, and generates structured reports.
🔍
Transparency
All verified complaints are publicly visible. Track every complaint from submission to resolution.
🏛️
Government Ready
Complaints are forwarded directly to relevant government departments with full AI analysis.

How It Works

Citizen Reports Issue
Upload photo + location + description or audio recording
AI Analyzes Complaint
Image verification, category classification, severity scoring, urgency detection
Moderation Review
50/50 cases are reviewed by human moderators for accuracy
Published to Dashboard
Verified complaints become publicly visible for transparency
Government Takes Action
Relevant department receives the AI-generated report and resolves the issue
