Aawaj (आवाज) means “Voice” in Nepali.

AAWAJ is an AI-powered civic complaint platform where citizens can report
real-world public issues in kathmadu valley such as:

• Broken roads / potholes
• Garbage problems
• Water leakage
• Electricity issues
• Health hazards
• School / education problems
• Corruption complaints
• Public infrastructure damage

Citizens simply upload image + auto location + optional text/audio in which between text and audion one is compulsory along with image and location.

System automatically analyzes the issue and forwards structured reports
to moderators and government dashboards.

        ↓
Uploads Complaint Image (mandatory)
        ↓
Adds Location (when user try to write location recommended location like google maps)
        ↓
Optional Text / Audio (one is mandatory)
        ↓
AI Analyzes Complaint
        ↓
Creates Smart Complaint Report
        ↓
Stored in Database
        ↓
Admin Moderation (50/50 chances of report requried admin approve)
        ↓
Published Public Dashboard(those report who Ai detets fullt and thos are not fake send to publc dashboard)
        ↓
Government Can Take Action (this is directlyconnected to the databses of that report who are send to public dashboard and AI makes clesr report of that report and send to government dash
which is connected in government offices)

    → LOW Confidence
